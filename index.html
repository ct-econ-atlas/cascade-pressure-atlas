What this is — and what this is not

This IS A narrative-class typology of currently-crystallized macro-narrative-engrams in financial discourse, with cascade-pressure scores between incompatible engram pairs. Predictions describe the FORM a cascade would take if a triggering signal landed. Forward predictions are pre-registered, locked, and scored at window-close.
This is NOT Financial advice. Asset positioning. Price prediction. A trading signal. A recommendation to buy, sell, hold, or position any asset. Readers who derive trading decisions from this document do so entirely at their own risk; the author and publisher accept no responsibility for such decisions.

1 · Crystallized Narrative-Engrams

Currently-active narrative-engrams in 2026-Q2 financial-discourse. Each is assigned one primary narrative-class label (T1..T17) and zero or more secondaries. Bridge-density counts the number of distinct discourse substrates the engram spans (1 = niche; 5 = saturating).

2 · Cascade-Pressure Map

Engram-pair tensions ranked by cascade-pressure score. The score combines sin²(Δθ) (narrative-incompatibility) with substrate-overlap and engram-rigidity. HIGH cascade-pressure pairs do NOT mean a cascade WILL trigger; they mean that IF a triggering signal lands, the cascade response will be larger.
